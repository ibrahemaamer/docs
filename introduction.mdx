## What is Nbula?

Nbula is a comprehensive platform that simplifies the process of building, deploying, and scaling AI applications. As a horizontal layer, Nbula enables developers to build up into full-fledged applications and down into infrastructure, allowing seamless integration at every level.

With Nbula, you can construct your base AI application in just eight clicks, reducing development time from months to mere days while maintaining enterprise-grade quality. This streamlined approach can save over $100K in development costs without compromising on performance.

## Core Features

- **Low-code AI Development** - Build, deploy, and scale AI applications with minimal coding
- **Plug-and-Play AI Models** - Integrate with OpenAI, LLaMA, Hugging Face, Langchain, and custom models
- **Prebuilt AI Components** - Leverage ready-made RAG systems, AI agents, and multi-modal workflows
- **Auto-scaling & Multi-cloud** - Optimize performance across AWS, GCP, Azure, or on-premises infrastructure
- **End-to-End Evaluation** - Benchmark performance and reduce AI failures with comprehensive testing

## Use Cases

Nbula supports a wide range of AI applications across industries:

- **Business Automation** - Document processing, HR automation, customer support
- **Finance & Risk** - Fraud detection, credit scoring, financial forecasting
- **Healthcare** - Medical document processing, assisted diagnosis, health assistants
- **Marketing & Sales** - Content generation, lead scoring, social media management
- **E-Commerce** - Recommendation engines, customer insights, visual search
- **Security & Compliance** - Anomaly detection, legal document analysis
- **Logistics & Supply Chain** - Demand forecasting, route optimization
- **Smart Cities & IoT** - Traffic management, energy optimization
- **Developer Tools** - Code generation, model versioning, A/B testing
